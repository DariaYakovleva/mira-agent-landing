Superpowers

What Mira does
for your community

Not just a bot — a member who reads everything, remembers everyone, and helps the right people find each other.

🧠
Remembers everything
Mira reads and indexes every message in your group. Ask her anything — she'll tell you who said what, when, and in what context.
Example: "Who was looking for a cosmetologist last week?" → Mira finds the exact message and person.
👥
Knows every member
Mira builds a profile of each participant based on what they write — their interests, expertise, needs, and activity level.
Example: "Who in the group is currently looking for a job?" → Mira gives you a list with context.
🔗
Connects the right people
When someone needs a contact, Mira proactively suggests who in the community can help — based on what people have written in the chat.
Example: Someone needs a lawyer → Mira: "@alex mentioned he works in corporate law, might be worth reaching out."
🔥
Sparks conversations
Mira asks engaging questions, reacts to silence, and keeps the discussion going — so you don't have to be the one always starting things.
Example: Chat goes quiet → Mira drops a relevant question based on recent topics discussed.
📊
Tracks activity & engagement
Know who's most active, who's gone quiet, and what topics drive the most engagement. Ask Mira for a community health report anytime.
Example: "Who are the top 5 contributors this month?" → instant answer.
💬
Available in DMs too
Members can ask Mira privately about the community — what's been discussed, who to talk to, what they missed. Like a personal concierge for your group.
Example: "What happened in the chat while I was away?" → Mira gives a personalized summary.
